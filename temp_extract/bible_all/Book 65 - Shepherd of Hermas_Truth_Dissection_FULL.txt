📘 BOOK 65: SHEPHERD OF HERMAS — Truth Dissection

📜 This file exposes manipulation, control myths, and occasional glimpses of divine clarity in a book often used to justify harsh tribalism. It is part of a full Bible dissection to uncover truth, strip away fear-based control, and rediscover what Yeshua truly taught: that the Kingdom of God is within us.

📖 Shepherd of Hermas, Vision 2:1-7
🔹 Text: I saw a woman clothed in bright garments, holding a book in her hand... and she told me to write what I saw and heard.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin | 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Allegorical woman as spirit of prophecy — mirrors Revelation but in gentler tones.

📖 Shepherd of Hermas, Commandment 5
🔹 Text: Be longsuffering and understanding... even if someone wrongs you, forgive without delay.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Direct moral teaching — no dogma, just clean-hearted ethics aligned with Yeshua.

📖 Shepherd of Hermas, Parable 9:12-16
🔹 Text: The tower was being built upon the waters... each stone tested and placed according to its worth...
🏷️ Tags: 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Mystical image of spiritual construction — symbolic of inner growth and divine order.

📖 Shepherd of Hermas, Vision 4:2-6
🔹 Text: An angel appeared as a shepherd, with a staff and light, and said: 'This is your guide for repentance.'
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Rooted in grace — salvation as a guided path, not a fear trap.

📖 Shepherd of Hermas, Commandment 12:2-6
🔹 Text: Do not let false spirits trouble you — test each one by its gentleness and peace.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin | 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: True discernment defined — emotional/spiritual resonance over rigid rules.
