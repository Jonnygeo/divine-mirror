📘 BOOK 74: PRAYER OF MANASSEH — Truth Dissection

📜 This file exposes manipulation, control myths, and occasional glimpses of divine clarity in a book often used to justify harsh tribalism. It is part of a full Bible dissection to uncover truth, strip away fear-based control, and rediscover what Yeshua truly taught: that the Kingdom of God is within us.

📖 Prayer of Manasseh 1:1-3
🔹 Text: O Lord Almighty... who made heaven and earth... who shut up the deep and sealed it by your terrible and glorious name...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Acknowledges divine power in creation — respectful awe, no fearmongering.

📖 Prayer of Manasseh 1:6-8
🔹 Text: You are patient and merciful, long-suffering and full of compassion... You promised repentance and forgiveness to those who have sinned against You.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin | 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Powerful admission of God’s true nature — mercy over wrath, love over punishment.

📖 Prayer of Manasseh 1:12-13
🔹 Text: I bow the knee of my heart, begging You for grace... I have sinned more than the number of the sands of the sea.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: One of the most raw confessions in Scripture — pure humility, no doctrine.

📖 Prayer of Manasseh 1:14
🔹 Text: I will praise You continually all the days of my life, for all the powers of heaven sing Your praise.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Ends in worship — showing repentance as transformation, not just apology.
