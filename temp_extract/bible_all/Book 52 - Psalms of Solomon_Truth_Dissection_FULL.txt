📘 BOOK 52: PSALMS OF SOLOMON — Truth Dissection

📜 This file exposes manipulation, control myths, and occasional glimpses of divine clarity in a book often used to justify harsh tribalism. It is part of a full Bible dissection to uncover truth, strip away fear-based control, and rediscover what Yeshua truly taught: that the Kingdom of God is within us.

📖 Psalm of Solomon 2:30-34
🔹 Text: Our enemies trampled us and cast us down... but You, O God, will raise up the one born of David...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Messianic expectation rooted in hardship — clearly foresees a future liberator with divine favor.

📖 Psalm of Solomon 3:1-8
🔹 Text: The righteous are not afraid... for God is with them and their hearts are firm...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Simple spiritual confidence — a powerful poetic vision of the awakened soul.

📖 Psalm of Solomon 8:23-30
🔹 Text: And they trusted in their wealth, and in their idols they made sacrifices...
🏷️ Tags: ⚠️ Human Manipulation
📝 Notes: Early condemnation of idolatry and material worship — timeless call against religious showmanship.

📖 Psalm of Solomon 11:1-6
🔹 Text: He will gather a holy people... He will not tolerate unrighteousness among them...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin | 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Speaks of divine selection through character, not bloodline — subversive in its time.

📖 Psalm of Solomon 17:21-31
🔹 Text: Behold, the Lord will raise up a king, the Son of David, to rule in righteousness and wisdom...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin | 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: One of the clearest non-Christian messianic prophecies — later linked directly to Yeshua.
