📘 BOOK 27: DANIEL — Truth Dissection (Selected from Chapters 1–12)

📜 This file exposes manipulation, control myths, and occasional glimpses of divine clarity in a book often used to justify harsh tribalism. It is part of a full Bible dissection to uncover truth, strip away fear-based control, and rediscover what Yeshua truly taught: that the Kingdom of God is within us.

📖 Daniel 1:17
🔹 Text: As for these four children, God gave them knowledge and skill in all learning and wisdom...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Highlights divine empowerment through discipline and faith — truth elevates, even in exile.

📖 Daniel 2:20-22
🔹 Text: Blessed be the name of God for ever and ever... he revealeth the deep and secret things...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin | 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Acknowledges God as the revealer of mysteries — core theme of divine knowledge over empire.

📖 Daniel 3:17-18
🔹 Text: If it be so, our God whom we serve is able to deliver us... but if not, be it known unto thee...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Courage rooted in faith. Choosing divine loyalty over fear or political power — echoes true spiritual freedom.

📖 Daniel 4:35
🔹 Text: He doeth according to his will... none can stay his hand, or say unto him, What doest thou?
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Recognition of divine sovereignty beyond human systems — humility before higher order.

📖 Daniel 7:13-14
🔹 Text: One like the Son of man came with the clouds of heaven...
🏷️ Tags: 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Visionary prophecy of a spiritual ruler. Later tied to Yeshua — imagery not meant for dogma but symbol of authority from above.

📖 Daniel 9:24-27
🔹 Text: Seventy weeks are determined... to make an end of sins...
🏷️ Tags: ⚠️ Human Manipulation
📝 Notes: Heavily interpreted and repurposed by institutions. Likely layered edits tied to post-exile religious narrative.

📖 Daniel 12:2-3
🔹 Text: Many of them that sleep in the dust of the earth shall awake...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin | 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Early seed of resurrection concept — points to spiritual awakening more than bodily doctrine.
