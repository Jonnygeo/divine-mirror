📘 BOOK 50: APOCALYPSE OF PETER — Truth Dissection

📜 This file exposes manipulation, control myths, and occasional glimpses of divine clarity in a book often used to justify harsh tribalism. It is part of a full Bible dissection to uncover truth, strip away fear-based control, and rediscover what Yeshua truly taught: that the Kingdom of God is within us.

📖 Apocalypse of Peter 3:2-6
🔹 Text: And the Lord showed me the place of the righteous... and their faces were bright with light...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: A rare peaceful heaven vision — focuses on joy, purity, and the reward of those truly awake.

📖 Apocalypse of Peter 5:1-6
🔹 Text: And I saw a great multitude in torment... and I said: Who are these, Lord? And He said: These are those who blasphemed the way of righteousness...
🏷️ Tags: ⚠️ Human Manipulation | ❌ Distortion
📝 Notes: Graphic depictions of torture — later Catholic-style hellfire likely influenced this version.

📖 Apocalypse of Peter 7:1-5
🔹 Text: But the Lord said: My righteous shall pray for them, and I will grant them rest in the end...
🏷️ Tags: 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Massive contradiction to eternal torment — speaks of restoration and redemption after correction.

📖 Apocalypse of Peter 8:1-4
🔹 Text: And He said: It is not my will that any should perish, but that all may turn and live...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Echoes Yeshua’s heart — aligns with the Gospel truth of mercy and inner transformation.

📖 Apocalypse of Peter 10:2-6
🔹 Text: And I saw some whose punishment ended... and the angels clothed them in white robes...
🏷️ Tags: 🌀 Hidden Truth | ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Powerful hint that even the 'condemned' are eventually restored — erased from the Roman version.
