📘 BOOK 62: APOCALYPSE OF ABRAHAM — Truth Dissection

📜 This file exposes manipulation, control myths, and occasional glimpses of divine clarity in a book often used to justify harsh tribalism. It is part of a full Bible dissection to uncover truth, strip away fear-based control, and rediscover what Yeshua truly taught: that the Kingdom of God is within us.

📖 Apocalypse of Abraham 8:1-6
🔹 Text: And the voice said to me: ‘Abraham, I am the God of the world... come out from your father’s house of idols’...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Radical call to spiritual separation — the first awakening moment in Abraham’s journey.

📖 Apocalypse of Abraham 9:1-7
🔹 Text: And I saw the altar fall and all the idols shatter... and I knew there was only one God...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin | 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Powerful rejection of idolatry — a core teaching hidden in the fog of tradition.

📖 Apocalypse of Abraham 17:1-8
🔹 Text: And I saw a great crowd before the throne... some on the right, shining with light, and others on the left in gloom...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Mystical vision of divine justice — matches other apocalyptic scenes like Matthew 25.

📖 Apocalypse of Abraham 23:1-6

🏷️ Tags: 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Prophetic glimpse of the Messiah — suppressed because it clearly precedes Yeshua.

📖 Apocalypse of Abraham 29:15-21
