📘 BOOK 9: 1 SAMUEL — Truth Dissection (Chapters 1–31)

📜 This file exposes manipulation, control myths, and occasional glimpses of divine clarity in a book often used to justify harsh tribalism. It is part of a full Bible dissection to uncover truth, strip away fear-based control, and rediscover what Yeshua truly taught: that the Kingdom of God is within us.

📖 1 Samuel 3:1-10
🔹 Text: The LORD called Samuel... Speak, for thy servant heareth.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: True prophetic calling. Clear example of divine communication. Childlike obedience and openness praised.

📖 1 Samuel 8:5-22
🔹 Text: Make us a king to judge us like all the nations... they have not rejected thee, but they have rejected me...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin | 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: God warns against monarchy. A rejection of divine rule for human hierarchy. Key pivot in Israel's spiritual decline.

📖 1 Samuel 10:17-25
🔹 Text: Ye have said... Nay, but a king shall reign over us... so Saul was chosen.
🏷️ Tags: ⚠️ Human Manipulation
📝 Notes: Despite warnings, people insist on king. God allows it, showing divine patience but also judgment by concession.

📖 1 Samuel 13:8-14
🔹 Text: Thou hast done foolishly... the LORD hath sought him a man after his own heart.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Saul disobeys spiritual protocol. Foreshadows David's rise. Highlights spiritual posture over position.

📖 1 Samuel 15:2-3
🔹 Text: Now go and smite Amalek... slay both man and woman, infant and suckling...
🏷️ Tags: ❌ Corrupt Addition
📝 Notes: Genocidal command. Highly suspect insertion used to justify violent conquest in the name of divine wrath.

📖 1 Samuel 16:7
🔹 Text: Man looketh on the outward appearance, but the LORD looketh on the heart.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Key spiritual truth. Heart-focused discernment. A central thread in Yeshua's message later on.

📖 1 Samuel 17:45-47
🔹 Text: Thou comest to me with a sword... but I come to thee in the name of the LORD...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin | 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: David vs Goliath. Archetypal story of faith, not fear. Symbolic of overcoming spiritual oppression with inner strength.

📖 1 Samuel 18:6-9
🔹 Text: Saul has slain his thousands, and David his ten thousands...
🏷️ Tags: ⚠️ Human Manipulation
📝 Notes: Comparison triggers Saul’s jealousy. Introduction of tribal favoritism and kingly pride rooted in ego.

📖 1 Samuel 24:10-12
🔹 Text: I will not put forth mine hand against my lord... the LORD judge between me and thee...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: David’s restraint and mercy. Spiritual maturity over vengeance. A lesson in humility and trust in divine justice.
