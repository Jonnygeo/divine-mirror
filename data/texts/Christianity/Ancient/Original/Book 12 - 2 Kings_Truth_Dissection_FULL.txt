📘 BOOK 12: 2 KINGS — Truth Dissection (Chapters 1–25)

📜 This file exposes manipulation, control myths, and occasional glimpses of divine clarity in a book often used to justify harsh tribalism. It is part of a full Bible dissection to uncover truth, strip away fear-based control, and rediscover what Yeshua truly taught: that the Kingdom of God is within us.

📖 2 Kings 2:1-14
🔹 Text: Elijah went up by a whirlwind into heaven... Elisha took up the mantle...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin | 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Spiritual succession symbolized by the mantle. Ascension imagery reflects elevation of consciousness and mission.

📖 2 Kings 4:1-7
🔹 Text: The oil stayed... pay thy debt, and live thou and thy children of the rest.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Miraculous provision with compassion. Reflects divine support for the humble and oppressed.

📖 2 Kings 5:1-14
🔹 Text: Naaman... dipped himself seven times... and his flesh came again...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Healing through faith and humility. Outsider blessed — reinforces that God honors those beyond religion.

📖 2 Kings 6:15-17
🔹 Text: They that be with us are more than they that be with them... and behold, the mountain was full of horses and chariots of fire...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin | 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Mystical vision of unseen forces. Spiritual reality surrounds us — echoed in Yeshua’s teaching on faith.

📖 2 Kings 17:7-20
🔹 Text: They served idols... they rejected his statutes... therefore the LORD was very angry...
🏷️ Tags: ⚠️ Human Manipulation
📝 Notes: Editorial theological justification for exile. Likely post-event rationalization of political collapse as divine punishment.

📖 2 Kings 18:3-7
🔹 Text: He trusted in the LORD... for he clave to the LORD, and departed not...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Hezekiah’s reform rooted in spiritual sincerity. Highlights power of personal conviction over national systems.

📖 2 Kings 19:14-19
🔹 Text: Thou art the God, even thou alone... incline thine ear, O LORD...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Prayer of desperation and faith. Shows heart posture of dependence, not ritual — a key spiritual anchor.

📖 2 Kings 21:1-16
🔹 Text: Manasseh did that which was evil... and shed innocent blood...
🏷️ Tags: ⚠️ Human Manipulation | ❌ Corrupt Addition
📝 Notes: Dark reign used to contrast reform. Possibly exaggerated to highlight moral decline and justify exile narrative.

📖 2 Kings 23:1-25
🔹 Text: Josiah read the book... and all the people stood to the covenant...
🏷️ Tags: 🌀 Hidden Truth | ⚠️ Human Manipulation
📝 Notes: Religious reform through rediscovered text. Spiritual renewal often tied to book-based revival — which can both liberate and control.

📖 2 Kings 25:1-21
🔹 Text: They slew the sons of Zedekiah before his eyes... and put out the eyes of Zedekiah...
🏷️ Tags: ⚠️ Human Manipulation
📝 Notes: Brutal ending to the kingdom. Likely used to cement fear-based legacy. Transition from monarchy to captivity justified theologically.
