📘 BOOK 20: PROVERBS — Truth Dissection (Selected from Chapters 1–31)

📜 This file exposes manipulation, control myths, and occasional glimpses of divine clarity in a book often used to justify harsh tribalism. It is part of a full Bible dissection to uncover truth, strip away fear-based control, and rediscover what Yeshua truly taught: that the Kingdom of God is within us.

📖 Proverbs 1:7
🔹 Text: The fear of the LORD is the beginning of knowledge...
🏷️ Tags: 🌀 Hidden Truth | ⚠️ Human Manipulation
📝 Notes: ‘Fear’ often mistranslated from awe/reverence. Rooted in spiritual respect, not terror — abused for control.

📖 Proverbs 3:5-6
🔹 Text: Trust in the LORD with all thine heart; and lean not unto thine own understanding...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Spiritual surrender and inner guidance. One of the clearest teachings on trusting divine flow.

📖 Proverbs 4:23
🔹 Text: Keep thy heart with all diligence; for out of it are the issues of life.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: One of the deepest wisdom lines in Scripture. Your internal state shapes your external world.

📖 Proverbs 8:22-31
🔹 Text: The LORD possessed me in the beginning... I was daily his delight...
🏷️ Tags: 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Wisdom personified before creation. Deep mystical language often tied to divine feminine or Logos.

📖 Proverbs 9:13-18
🔹 Text: A foolish woman is clamorous... her guests are in the depths of hell.
🏷️ Tags: ❌ Corrupt Addition | ⚠️ Human Manipulation
📝 Notes: Sexist overtones and fear-based messaging. Later rabbinic influence likely added to reinforce patriarchy.

📖 Proverbs 11:25
🔹 Text: The liberal soul shall be made fat: and he that watereth shall be watered also himself.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Spiritual generosity and reciprocity. Echoes universal truth — what you give, returns.

📖 Proverbs 16:18
🔹 Text: Pride goeth before destruction, and an haughty spirit before a fall.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Timeless law of humility. A reminder echoed by Yeshua and many sages across traditions.

📖 Proverbs 22:6
🔹 Text: Train up a child in the way he should go...
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Core parental principle rooted in alignment — not force. Planting seeds of truth and wisdom early.

📖 Proverbs 31:10-31
🔹 Text: Who can find a virtuous woman?... her children arise up, and call her blessed...
🏷️ Tags: 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Honors feminine strength and spiritual character. Genuine praise of wisdom through a mother’s legacy.
