📘 BOOK 71: WISDOM OF SOLOMON — Truth Dissection

📜 This file exposes manipulation, control myths, and occasional glimpses of divine clarity in a book often used to justify harsh tribalism. It is part of a full Bible dissection to uncover truth, strip away fear-based control, and rediscover what Yeshua truly taught: that the Kingdom of God is within us.

📖 Wisdom of Solomon 1:6-7
🔹 Text: Wisdom is a spirit that loves mankind... For the spirit of the Lord fills the world and that which holds all things together has knowledge of every sound.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Profound and pure — echoes quantum spiritual truth about vibrational connection.

📖 Wisdom of Solomon 2:23-24
🔹 Text: God created man to be immortal... but by the envy of the devil, death entered the world.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin | 🌀 Hidden Truth
📝 Notes: Early explanation of spiritual death — consistent with Eden, but framed with clarity and no shame.

📖 Wisdom of Solomon 3:1-9
🔹 Text: The souls of the righteous are in the hand of God... they are at peace... and shall shine like sparks through the stubble.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Vision of immortality beyond fear — rejected later for being too grace-based and universal.

📖 Wisdom of Solomon 6:11-13
🔹 Text: Desire wisdom, and she will protect you... watch for her early, and you will find her sitting at your gate.
🏷️ Tags: ✅ Divine Origin
📝 Notes: Wisdom as feminine divine presence — ancient truth buried beneath later masculine-only theology.

📖 Wisdom of Solomon 15:7-8
🔹 Text: A potter kneads soft clay and forms vessels... but vainly hopes to make a god out of the work of his hands...
🏷️ Tags: 🌀 Hidden Truth | ⚠️ Human Manipulation
📝 Notes: Symbolic attack on idol worship — but also warns about religious systems deifying their own creation.
